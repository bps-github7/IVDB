Information about the Internet Videogame Database

Thanks for visiting the site! We are here to give you up to date and accurate data about Videogames

Generally, there are two broad categoires of data which IVDB deals with:

  1. Content: official posts of the IVDB, including but not limited to- news, streaming information, watchlists and official posts such as games and game info
  2. Contributions: content produced by regular users and above, which includes comments, forum posts, ratings, reviews and suggestions
